About Our Program

The X Program is a 6-month journey for high school graduates preparing for university. Each week, students explore self-awareness, life skills, and leadership to ensure they transition confidently into higher education.

We combine mentorship, practical workshops, and digital literacy to equip students with lifelong learning skills aligned to SDG 4: Quality Education.
